Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CloudSuite

How long is the free trial?

Our free trial lasts for 14 days. You'll have full access to all features of your selected plan during this period. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We also offer two-factor authentication, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and other regulations.

Can I import data from other tools?

Yes, CloudSuite supports importing data from most popular project management and collaboration tools. Our setup wizard will guide you through the process, or our support team can assist you with custom imports.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for nonprofits, educational institutions, and startups. Please contact our sales team for more information about our discount programs.
