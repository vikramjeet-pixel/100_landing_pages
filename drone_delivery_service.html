How It Works

1

Request a Delivery

Use our app or website to schedule a pickup. Enter package details, destination, and preferred delivery time.

2

Package Pickup

Our drone arrives at your location. Place your package in the secure compartment and confirm the pickup.

3

Aerial Transport

The drone navigates to the destination using advanced GPS and obstacle avoidance systems, taking the most efficient route.

4

Safe Delivery

The drone lands at the delivery location. The recipient receives a notification and unlocks the compartment using a unique code.
